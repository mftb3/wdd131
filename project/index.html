Pawprints Chronicles: Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Pawprints Chronicles

The name reflects the unique and cherished stories of two family dogs, highlighting their adventures, personalities, and bond with the family. It's designed to resonate with dog lovers everywhere.

Optional Domain Availability: pawprintschronicles.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Pawprints Chronicles is to celebrate the lives of two family dogs by sharing their stories, photos, and videos. The site will provide a platform for dog lovers to connect, share tips, and learn about caring for family pets. It will include a blog, photo gallery, and a section for dog care tips.

Scenarios

  • What are the stories of Rocky and Charlie, the two family dogs featured on the site?
  • Where can I find helpful tips for caring for my own dogs?
  • How do I connect with other dog lovers through the site?

Color Schema

The selected colors for the site are:

  • Primary Color: #FF9800 (used for headings and buttons).
  • Accent Color: #F5F5F5 (used for backgrounds).
  • Secondary Accent: #8BC34A (used for links and call-to-action areas).

Typography

The selected fonts for the site are:

  • Headings: "Pacifico", cursive (to evoke playfulness and warmth).
  • Body Text: "Roboto", sans-serif (for readability and modernity).
